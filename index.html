About Konel Hotel

Where luxury meets exceptional service

Founded in 2020, Konel Hotel has established itself as the premier luxury destination in the heart of the city of DIRE DAWA. Our commitment to excellence has earned us numerous awards and the loyalty of discerning travelers from around the world.

Every detail at Konel Hotel is carefully curated to provide an unforgettable experience. From our architecturally stunning building to our meticulously trained staff, we strive to exceed expectations at every turn.
